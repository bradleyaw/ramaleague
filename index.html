The Basics

  • Location

    Rama Swim Club - Address: 6109 Wheeler Dr, Charlotte, NC 28211

  • Cost

    $110 total. $100 goes to Rama for a yearly membership. This fee covers two or three leagues + access to courts. The other $10(pay Alex or Rob) is so we can give out prizes at least twice yearly.

    Pay your Rama fee here - Select "Membership: Individual"
  • About Rama

    Rama is a private swim club that has a swim team, private events, and has other volleyball happenings. The community is fairly large, but we have the exclusive use of the courts on Monday nights for our coed league, and Thursdays for our mens league. There is free 4s BB level pickup on Tuesdays that starts around 6:30, Wednesday is open coed play B-A level typically, and Friday is a 4s League. Weekends are first come first serve to members, with few exceptions.

  • Membership Info

    Membership grants access to the volleyball courts for anyone that pays the $100 fee. We also get access to the pool on Memorial and Labor day weekends.

  • Guest Info

    Subs for leagues are always permitted, however on weekends or other nights, if someone is interested in joining, they may come and play once however, repeat guests are not permitted.

  • Ball of Choice

    AVP Ball. Any of the last 3 iterations based off what players bring. Other Rama leagues use the old spalding.
