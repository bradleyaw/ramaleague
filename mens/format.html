Mens Format

  • Format

    King of the Beach. 3 games per night, then free play

  • Number of Players

    15 total. 12 per week, with 3 subs each week. 4 per court.

  • Start time

    With 12 people and 2 courts, the top 8 players will being games at 6:15. The bottom 4 start theirs at 7:15 or whenever the first court opens. The second court is free play all night.

  • Can't make a week?

    Each week 3 people have byes. You can request specific byes if a slot is open, but if not, find your own sub. If having trouble reach out to the group chat.

  • Standings

    Standings are first determined by wins per week. Tiebreakers go to the higher point differential, and then more games played.

  • League Length

    Each "League" is 10(2 of which are byes) Weeks of play. If we miss a Thursday to rain, it does not count as a week. Last year we got in 2 full Leagues and one ~6 week quickie at the end as it got colder.
